Meet Sweet!








Need some ideas on what kind of activity to do? Fear not! We've compiled some ideas for each category over here :) Scroll down and read on!


Readers

What book genres do each of you read? Fantasy, sci-fi, nonfiction... there's a lot to go around! Try seeing where your interests overlap, and picking out a book to read - sort of like a mini book club! :)


Physical Activity

There's a lot of sports and exercises to go around! Try doing a one-on-one game or race, or perhaps a workout together!


Music

While everyone may have different tastes, you two definitely love to listen to your tunes 24/7! Try making a playlist of your favorite songs and share it with your partner - who knows, they may find their next favorite song!


Watching Movies

Check out what's playing at your local theater, or perhaps invite them over to find something good on Netflix or another streaming platform! Get the snacks ready, too!


Hanging Out

Anything that doesn't fit into the aforementioned categories goes here :) You two love to spend time chilling, so try going out for a coffee or a snack, or maybe to one of Cupertino's beautiful parks :)
